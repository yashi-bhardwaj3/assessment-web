A Pie chart is a circular statistical graphics, which is divided into slices to illustrate numerical proportion. This Pie chart shows the comparison between the sales of the product through different medium such as Online Sales, In store sales etc.

The Doughnut Chart is a built-in chart type in Excel. Doughnut charts are meant to express a "part-to-whole" relationship, where all pieces together represent 100%. This Doughnut chart shows the comparison between the sales of the product through different medium such as Online Sales, In store sales etc in past 3 years.

Polar area charts are similar to pie charts, but each segment has the same angle - the radius of the segment differs depending on the value. This Polar area chart shows the comparison between the sales of the product through different medium such as Online Sales, In store sales etc.
